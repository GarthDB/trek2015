Purposes of the Trek

A pioneer trek is a special opportunity for youth to have spiritual experiences where they gain a deeper appreciation for pioneers who forged a path before them. It is a type of youth conference where youth can grow their testimonies, gain appreciation for one’s ancestors and the pioneers, increase their self worth by preparing spiritually and physically and knowing they can accomplish hard things, gain new friends and build stronger peer relationships in a safe but challenging setting (see Handbook 2 13.4 and Church Youth Handcart Trek Guidelines).
